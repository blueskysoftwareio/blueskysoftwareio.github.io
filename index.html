About Blue Sky Software

Blue Sky Software is a boutique Salesforce consultancy founded by Kris Sparks, a seasoned Salesforce Technical Architect with deep expertise across the platform.

We partner with businesses to design, build, and optimize Salesforce solutions that drive real results. From complex Apex and Lightning Web Component development to solution architecture and strategic consulting — we bring senior-level expertise to every engagement.

As a boutique firm, we offer the technical depth of a large consultancy with the agility and personal attention of a dedicated partner. Every project is led directly by a senior architect — no handoffs, no layers, no surprises.
